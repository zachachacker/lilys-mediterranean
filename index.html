Our Table · Since 2020

A little corner of the Mediterranean, two blocks from the Atlantic.

Lily's is a family kitchen — the kind where recipes come from memory, not a manual, and the grill has been going since we opened our doors in Indialantic in 2020.

We cook the Lebanese and Mediterranean food we grew up on: hummus whipped by hand, marinades left overnight, lamb and chicken char-grilled to order over open flame. Our kitchen is open, so you can watch your plate come together.

Everything is fresh and never frozen — no shortcuts, no freezer aisle. Regulars call it "the best Mediterranean in Brevard," and honestly, we cook like we're feeding family, because most days that's exactly how it feels.

— Lily & family

Why it tastes like home

Three things we won't cut corners on.

01

Fresh, never frozen

Produce, meat and herbs come in fresh and get prepped in-house. Nothing thaws out of a bag on the way to your plate.

02

Grilled to order

Skewers hit the open flame when you order them — not before. That's why the char is right and the lamb is still juicy.

03

Family-run hospitality

You're likely to be served by the owner herself. It's a small family kitchen, and it shows in every plate that leaves it.
